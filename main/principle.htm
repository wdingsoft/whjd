Elder Care Agreement

(Wei&Hang) Version 2022-08

General Principle

  1. The elder care principle is established based on the fifth Moses Law: Honor your parents. It's neither following nor against the elder's will but respect and care for them based upon the love of the Lord.
  2. The elder care is composed of two parts: human care and asset care. Hunam care includes physical health care and spiritual health care; Asset care includes finance and housing management.
  3. Hereof, the elder care refer to the care of mom in her whole retirement life both in short or long term, including her daily life, emergency care, ultimate care, spiritual care, assets management.
  4. The elder care is a 24 hours full time job.
  5. An elder care report shall be published monthly for family and relatives to share.

The Elder Care Duties

  1. Show love of the Lord to the elder.
  2. Make the elder to be happy
  3. Provide special soft food for elders daily.
  4. Accompany the elder where she goes.
  5. Hiking with her often.
  6. Find healthy entertaiments.
  7. Find a healthy job for the elder.
  8. Help the elder to fit in the new environment.
  9. Help the elder to learn English to communicate with neighbors.
  10. Resolve conflictions in the family.
  11. Pay attention the gas stove whenever she uses.
  12. Assign adquate jobs (planting, pet care, etc)
  13. Provide appropiate information.
  14. Room Temperature:97

  3. The output of saving is used for elder care covering:

    2. medical fees.
    3. nanny/helpers cost.
    4. daily food, clothes,
    5. medicines, nutritions.
    6. house utility, maintemance fees.

    8. Some visiting fees.

  5. Spending for elder care shall follow the economic rules: frugal and effecient.

  11. The person who lives with and cares the elder shall should be primiarily responsible for the elder care operation.

House Management Principle

  1. The parents' house is merely used for mom.
  2. In case of being sold out, the money should belong to mom.

Investment Principle

  1. To keep or increase the value of the parents' property/asset is an important part of elder care.
  2. It is the mom's wish that children should open a private company or coorperation.
  3. Under the condition that mom's emergency care will not be affected, the mom's saving/property can be invested (house, stock, mutual found, etc) prudentially. But all income/profits will belong to the mom; Any loss belongs to all family.
  4. Any investment shall be approved ahead before execution.
