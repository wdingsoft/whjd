Elder Care Agreement

(Wei&Hang) Version 2022-08

General Principle

  1. The elder care principle is established based on the fifth Moses Law: Honor your parents. It's neither following nor against the elder's will but respect and care for them based upon the love of the Lord.
  2. The elder care is composed of two parts: human care and asset care. Hunam care includes physical health care and spiritual health care; Asset care includes finance and housing management.
  3. Hereof, the elder care refer to the care of mom in her whole retirement life both in short or long term, including her daily life, emergency care, ultimate care, spiritual care, assets management.
  4. The elder care is a 24 hours full time job.
  5. An elder care report shall be published monthly for family and relatives to share.

The Elder Care Duties

  1. Show love of the Lord to the elder.
  2. Make the elder to be happy
  3. Provide special soft food for elders daily.
  4. Accompany the elder where she goes.
  5. Hiking with her often.
  6. Find healthy entertaiments.
  7. Find a healthy job for the elder.
  8. Help the elder to fit in the new environment.
  9. Help the elder to learn English to communicate with neighbors.
  10. Resolve conflictions in the family.
  11. Pay attention the gas stove whenever she uses.
  12. Assign adquate jobs (planting, pet care, etc)
  13. Provide appropiate information.
  14. Room Temperature:97

Financial Care Rules

  1. The initial summarized financial foundation is CNY3,275,177.28 and $6782.74. (refer to xls report(2022/09/01)
  2. The income is about CNY7000 per month from pension and interests.
  3. The foundation and income are used for elder care, based upon the agreement, that covers:
    1. The hospital nursing/emergency fees.
    2. The medical fees.
    3. The nanny/helpers cost.
    4. The medicines, nutritions.
    5. The daily food, clothes,
    6. The house utility, maintemance fees.
    7. All kinds of social service fees, including the related notary, visa/gc application fee, agent consultant fees, as well as related transportation fees.
    8. Some visiting fees.
  4. A minimum of emergency currency ($100,000.00) is available on site where she lives.
  5. Spending for elder care shall follow the economical rules: optimal frugal and effecient.
  6. Overall elder care expense should be limited within the capacity of the mom's saving account as much as possible to avoid children's burden. If parents account runs out of capacity, all children should contribute and support it equally.
  7. Any expense greater than $1000.0 (cny6,000.0) need be recorded and documented in the monthly expense report database.
  8. Any expense greater than $10,000.0 (cny60,000.0) need be agreed/approved by all children prior to execution to avoid family misunderstanding unless life risk or medical emergency.
  9. For simplicity or operational purpose, the money may go through different' banks under different personal names. But the overall value should not be changed.
  10. Overall expense, balance/statust shall be published in category monthtly or quaterly.
  11. The person who lives with and cares the elder shall should be primiarily responsible for the elder care operation.

House Management Principle

  1. The parents' house is merely used for mom.
  2. In case of house being sold out, its value should be added in the foundation for elder care.

Financial Management Principle

  1. Under the condition that the emergency care will not be affected, it is a duty for elder care to keep the value from depreciation.
  2. It is the elder's wish o keep or increase the value of the property/asset as much as possible.
  3. Any financial management risk shall be evalucated and agreed before execution.
